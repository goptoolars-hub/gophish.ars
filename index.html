This is a Phishing Simulation undertaken by AuthBridge as a part of its security awareness exercise. No real credentials were collected.

Phishing Email Red Flags:

  • Sender email ID doesn’t match the official support email ID.
  • Urgent or threatening language in the subject/body.
  • Links that don’t go to the official site.

Always verify before you click. When in doubt, Contact IT Team .
